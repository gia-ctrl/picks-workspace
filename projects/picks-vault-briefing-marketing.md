# Picks Vault — Marketing Briefing

> This document is a complete briefing on the Picks Vault model. Use it to build a marketing strategy and launch plan.

---

## What is Picks?

Picks is a sports betting platform focused on **player props** (e.g., "Mbappe scores", "Vinicius 2+ assists") running on Base (Ethereum L2). Currency: USDC.

**Launch target:** World Cup 2026 (June-July 2026).

---

## What is the Vault?

The Vault is the **ownership model** of the product. It allows anyone to become "the house" by depositing USDC.

**How it works:**
- Every bet on the platform runs against the Vault
- The Vault earns the **hold** (~20% of total wagered amount on average)
- LP (Liquidity Provider) depositors own shares in the Vault
- Share price rises as the Vault profits from betting activity

**This is not yield farming or staking.** This is tokenized ownership of a casino's house edge — backed by real revenue from real bets.

---

## Three Participants

| Role | What they do | What they earn |
|---|---|---|
| **Players** | Bet on player props | Payouts when they win |
| **Vault LPs** | Deposit USDC, fund the other side of bets | ~75% of the hold, via share price appreciation |
| **Referrers** | Bring players to the platform | ~10% of bets + winnings from referrals |

**Picks (the company)** earns:
1. As an LP on its own $250K seed capital
2. 15% protocol fee on all hold

## Vault Structure

### Per League, Per Season
Each Vault is tied to a specific competition:
- 🏆 World Cup 2026 Vault (first launch)
- ⚽ Champions League 26/27 Vault (future)
- 🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿 Premier League 26/27 Vault (future)

When a tournament ends → Vault settles → LPs withdraw profits → New Vault opens next season. **Every season is a new drop.**

### Share-Based System
- LP deposits USDC → receives shares at current price
- Share price starts at $1.00
- As Vault profits → share price rises
- Early depositors get shares cheaper = more upside
- Late depositors pay higher share price = less upside, less risk

**Example:**
```
Day 1:   LP-A deposits $10,000 → 10,000 shares @ $1.00
Day 15:  Vault up 8% → share price = $1.08
         LP-B deposits $10,000 → 9,259 shares @ $1.08
End:     Share price = $1.22
         LP-A profit: +22%
         LP-B profit: +13%
```

### Key Rules (V1 — World Cup)
- **No cap** on Vault size (more capital = more betting activity supported)
- **Deposits open** before and during the tournament
- **Withdrawals locked** until tournament ends
- **Real-time P&L** visible to all LPs

## Competitive Positioning

### What we studied:

**Megapot** (megapot.io) — Daily on-chain lottery with a similar 3-player model (Players/Backers/Referrers). Backers fund prize pool, earn ~20% hold. Share-based LP system. $200M+ in drawings, $5M raised from Dragonfly/Coinbase Ventures/FanDuel founders.

**Pixie Chess** (pixiechess.xyz) — Chess + collectible NFT pieces on Base. Vault funded by piece sales → tournament prizes. VRGDA dynamic pricing. Seasonal structure.

### How Picks is different:
- **Skill-based market** (sports knowledge matters) vs. pure luck (lottery/chess)
- **Real sporting events** — World Cup, Champions League — built-in global audience
- **Seasonal vault renewal** — creates recurring engagement, not one-time deposit
- **Share appreciation model** — closest thing to owning stock in a casino
