World Cup History

Pulisic made his World Cup debut at Qatar 2022, scoring the decisive goal against Iran that sent the USMNT through to the Round of 16. He was injured on the play, crashing into the goalkeeper, and missed the start of the Netherlands knockout match. He finished the tournament with 1 goal and 1 assist in 3 appearances.

At 27, Pulisic enters 2026 in his prime and as the undisputed face of American soccer. A home World Cup is the stage he's been building toward since breaking through at Borussia Dortmund as a teenager.
