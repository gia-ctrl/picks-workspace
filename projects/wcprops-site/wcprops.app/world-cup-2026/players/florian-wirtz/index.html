World Cup History

Mbappe burst onto the World Cup stage at Russia 2018, becoming the youngest French scorer since Just Fontaine and drawing comparisons to Pele. He scored 4 goals as France won their second title. In Qatar 2022, he was even more devastating: 8 goals including a hat-trick in the final against Argentina, winning the Golden Boot.

His 12 goals in 14 World Cup appearances is an extraordinary rate. Now at Real Madrid and in his prime at 27, Mbappe enters 2026 as the consensus Golden Boot favorite and France's talisman in their quest for a third title.
