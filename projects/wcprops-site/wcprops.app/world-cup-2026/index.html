FAQ

When does the 2026 World Cup start?
The 2026 FIFA World Cup kicks off on June 11, 2026 at MetLife Stadium in New Jersey. The final is July 19, 2026 at the same venue.
How many teams are in the 2026 World Cup?
48 teams, expanded from 32, divided into 12 groups of 4. The largest FIFA World Cup in history.
What are player props?
Predictions on individual player performance: goals, assists, cards, shots on target. Unlike match bets, props focus on what specific players will do. Player props let you use real data — stats, form, xG — to make smarter predictions on individual performance.
Who are the favorites to win?
Top favorites include Argentina (defending champions), France, England, Brazil, and host nation USA. Home advantage historically gives co-hosts a significant boost.
Where will the 2026 World Cup be played?
Co-hosted by USA, Canada, and Mexico across 16 venues including MetLife Stadium (NY/NJ), SoFi Stadium (LA), AT&T Stadium (Dallas), Estadio Azteca (Mexico City), and BMO Field (Toronto).
How is Picks different from sportsbooks?
Player props platforms let you predict individual player performance instead of game outcomes. Markets are P2P — transparent odds, verifiable results, no house edge. You play against other users, not against the house.
