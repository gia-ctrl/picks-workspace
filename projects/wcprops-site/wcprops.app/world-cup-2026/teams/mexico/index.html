World Cup History

Mexico have appeared in 17 FIFA World Cups and have reached the quarterfinals twice (1970 and 1986), both as host nation.

From 1994 through 2018, Mexico advanced to the Round of 16 in seven consecutive tournaments, one of the longest such runs in modern World Cup history.
